Frequently
Asked Questions

How can earn points in wick?

Its easy. You can post your old books to wick we will give you 50% of book price as wicoins. You can purchase wicoins using your credit/debit card and using netbanking as well. Know more

Can I get my book back?

Yes, You will get you books back only if you selected Return Needed option while posting your book. Otherwise you won’t get your books back once you shared with other user. Your books will travel miles and miles. When a reader reads your book you will get 1 wicoins as bonus.

How much time I can hold a book?

Its depends on you, You can take your own time to read a book. Once you completed reading you can mark book as reading over.
